Brightening roots

Turmeric brings warmth to uneven-looking skin.

Known for its golden color and long history in natural care, turmeric helps tell the brand story of visible glow without harshness. It fits routines focused on dullness, post-breakout marks, and uneven tone.

Calm and clarify

Neem supports skin that needs a gentler reset.

For customers dealing with breakouts or irritation, neem gives the ingredient story a calmer chapter: purifying, botanical, and chosen for skin that needs support without feeling stripped.
